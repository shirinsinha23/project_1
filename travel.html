Mumbai, Maharashtra

Mumbai,also know as Bombay,is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra.Mumbai is the financial capital and the most populous city proper of India with an estimated population of 12.5 million (1.25 crore). Mumbai is the centre of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region, the sixth-most populous metropolitan area in the world with a population of over 23 million (2.3 crore).

Why I love Mumbai:

  • The rich history,colonial-era architecture.
  • delicious street food,including vada pav and pav bhaji
  • The Bollywood film industry and artistic cultures
  • Beautiful beaches and stunning Marine Drive

New Delhi,India

New Delhi, the capital of India, is a city that blends history and modernity seamlessly. It is home to magnificent monuments like India Gate, Red Fort, and Qutub Minar, as well as bustling markets and diverse cuisine.

Why I love New Delhi:

  • Historical landmarks and stunning Mughal architecture.
  • A diverse food scene with delicious North Indian dishes.
  • Vibrant markets like Chandni Chowk and Connaught Place.
  • The rich cultural and political significance.
